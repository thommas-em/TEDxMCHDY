About us...

TEDxMarie Curie Ha Dong Youth is a student-run organization that is dedicated to spreading ideas and sparking conversations. We are a group of students who are passionate about sharing ideas and creating a community of thinkers and doers. Our goal is to inspire and empower people to make a positive impact on the world around them. We believe that everyone has the power to change the world, and we want to help them do it.
